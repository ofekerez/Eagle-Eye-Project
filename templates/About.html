About Us

 
    Eagle Eye is a software made to help network engineers and penetration testers scan the networks they need to examine efficiently
    and easily. The project contains many tools made in order to simplify and ease the work of examining
    and testing a network's security. This project was developed by Ofek Erez as the CyberSecurity project for the
    Computer Science major.
